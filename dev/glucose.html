Low Blood Sugar

⚡ Immediate Steps:

  1. Eat 15g fast-acting carbs (3-4 glucose tablets, 1/2 cup juice, 1 tbsp honey)

  5. Seek emergency care if symptoms worsen or you cannot safely treat yourself
