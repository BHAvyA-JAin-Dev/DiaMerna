📖 Dropbox Setup Guide
📦 Dropbox One-Tap OAuth
The app owner has already configured Dropbox. To connect your account:

1. Make sure you're logged in to your Dropbox account in the browser
2. Click "🔗 Connect to Dropbox" above
3. Authorize the DiaMerna app (App folder access only)
4. You'll be redirected back automatically

The Dropbox app uses App folder permission type — files are stored in a dedicated DiaMerna/YourName/ folder, not your entire Dropbox. Only the app can access this folder.
